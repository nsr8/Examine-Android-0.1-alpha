About the app

This app is intended for teaching, learning, and practicing clinical examination of patients. Target audience is doctors, medical students, nurses, nursing students and anybody who wants to learn clinical examination.

 It is made available free without any assurance of the correctness or comprehensiveness of the content. The developer is not in any manner responsible for consequences of the use of this app.

Feedback

Your feedback is always appreciated. Please send your general feedback and feature request to mnzuhair@gmail.com

Contribute

Your contribution is essential for the continued development and maintenance of the app. If you would like to contribute to the app with written content, correcting subject matter, or would like to correct any thing wrong in the app, please email me.

Please report any bugs on the Issues section of the GitHub page at https://github.com/nsr8/Examine-Android/issues. You are more than welcome to fork the app and to commit changes!
